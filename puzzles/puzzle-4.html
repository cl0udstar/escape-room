Mission 4

Accessibility

To get past Evelyn Scott and get to the base without her chasing you down, you must find the quickest route. In the wild weather, you must avoid the areas of the water with strong winds to ensure maximum speed. There are 10 checkpoints where you have to make a decision between wind speed 1 and wind speed 2. You can change between the blue and orange paths at each checkpoint, to find the least windy route.

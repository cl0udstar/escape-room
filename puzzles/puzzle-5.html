Mission 5

Accessibility

To unlock the password, you need to follow the steps below:

Step 1: Order the Fjordhelm locations in order of visitors per month, from highest to lowest.

  • 680 Visitors
  • 900 Visitors
  • 830 Visitors
  • 1000 Visitors
  • 740 Visitors
  • 280 Visitors
  • 400 Visitors
  • 515 Visitors
  • 595 Visitors
  • 200 Villagers

Step 2: Match each location to its letter from the map

Step 3: In the same order from step one, spell out the letters, in order of visitors per month.

Step 4: Using the letter sequence from 'Step 3', match the letters from the 1st box, with the letters in the second box.
